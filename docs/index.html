How to use it

  1. Drag the link into your Bookmarks Bar
  2. Go to a web site and enjoy reading something
  3. Scroll down… more reading, goes up… and when ads appears...
  4. Clic your bookmark’s link created in point 1.
  5. Whalla! No more ads, it’s gone!
  6. If.. some ads are still on page, please report them here!

Ps: if you want to reset the site, just press “F5” to refresh the site, and the ADS will be there again for you.

How its works

When you press the button to "Hide Ads", you are calling this style (css file), which simply "hides" from your web browser all the ads that the page shows each time we are reading content.
